Kashi Vishwanath Temple

“Kashi Vishwanath Temple undisputedly comes first amongst all the major temples of Varanasi. It is placed as the 11th Jyotirlingas out of 12 located across the different states of India, where the lord Shiva resides in the form of Visweswar Jyotirlinga. Kashi is the ancient name of Varanasi whereas Vishwanath is one of the several names of Shiva, and by clubbing both the names together the temple has got its name. The temple is located quite near to the most popular and momentous GHAT called as Dashashwamedh ghatwhere devotees take bath by dipping into the holy river Ganga before getting into the temple’s premises to perform the worship of God Shiva. In order to reach the Kashi Vishwanath temple Varanasi, you have to way through a narrow and crowded street market. The street is known as Vishwanath Gali which represents the traditional old market formed by a row of small shops positioned at both the sides of street. You will find people queuing up for hours to enter the temple, the best time to visit the temple is early morning. The vibes are amazing XD, And Don`t forget to enjoy 'Maliyo' dessert available in streets.”
