About IIT BHU ka OLX

IIT-BHU Insights brings a platform to exchange needs of people all over campus.
Fresher's can lent/buy/contact the seniors for their needs and Seniors can sell their bikes, books and can get a good deal.

We came up with this very idea as fresher's require bicycles/bikes, books and seniors require to sell the items or accommodate them which are not of use any more.
Boom why not we help each other in this exchange, so here we get you covered.
